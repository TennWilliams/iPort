About

I am an ambitious Associate Software Engineer who worked diligently to venture into the Technology field. I was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY and graduated from Pace University with a Masters in Computer Science and a minor in Information Systems. As life may have it, after graduation I didn't land a job in my field so I settled for a career in Insurance. After years of working as an analyst I started my pursuit of becoming a Software Engineer by enrolling into MIT certification course, specializing in FullStack Javascript Programming with MERN. Six months into the course I accepted a position at Travelers Insurance as an Associate Software Engineer.
